Grilled Chicken Breast

A delicious grilled chicken breast for lunch!

Grilled chicken breast is a simple yet delicious recipe that's perfect for muscle gain. This high-protein dish is a go-to for fitness enthusiasts. To make it, season boneless, skinless chicken breasts with your choice of herbs and spices. Then, grill or pan-sear them until they are perfectly cooked. The result is tender, juicy chicken with a smoky flavor that's hard to resist. Serve it with a side of steamed vegetables and brown rice for a well-rounded meal that provides the essential nutrients needed for muscle growth and recovery. Whether you're a seasoned chef or new to the kitchen, grilled chicken breast is a quick and easy recipe that should be in your regular meal rotation.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Your choice of herbs and spices (e.g., salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika)
  • Olive oil (for grilling)
  • Steamed vegetables (for serving)
  • Brown rice (for serving)

Steps

  1. Preheat the Grill:

    2. Start by preheating your grill to medium-high heat. If you're using a stovetop grill pan, heat it over medium-high heat.

  2. Season the Chicken:

    3. While the grill is heating, season the boneless, skinless chicken breasts with your choice of herbs and spices. You can create a simple seasoning mix by combining salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika. Rub the seasoning onto both sides of the chicken.

  3. Oil the Grill:

    4. Once the grill is hot, brush it with a little olive oil to prevent the chicken from sticking.

  4. Grill the Chicken:

    5. Place the seasoned chicken breasts on the grill. Grill for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C) and the chicken is no longer pink in the center. The cooking time may vary depending on the thickness of the chicken breasts.

  5. Serve:

    6. Once the chicken is fully cooked and has beautiful grill marks, remove it from the grill. Let it rest for a few minutes before serving.

  6. Prepare the Sides:

    7. While the chicken is resting, prepare your side dishes. Steam some vegetables and cook brown rice according to package instructions.

  7. Enjoy:

    8. Serve the grilled chicken breast with steamed vegetables and a portion of brown rice. This balanced meal provides the essential nutrients needed for muscle growth and recovery.
