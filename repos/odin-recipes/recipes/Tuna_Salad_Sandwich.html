Tuna Salad Sandwich

A delicious and healthy tuna salad sandwich!

Tuna salad sandwich is a delectable and nutritious choice for a satisfying meal. This recipe features a flavorful tuna salad made with canned tuna, mayo, celery, and various seasonings. The tuna salad is generously spread between two slices of your favorite bread, and it's paired with fresh lettuce and tomato for a refreshing crunch. This sandwich is a fantastic source of protein and healthy fats, making it an ideal option for a quick and fulfilling lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • Canned tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Celery
  • Seasonings (e.g., salt, pepper, paprika)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Bread slices

Steps

  1. Open the canned tuna and drain any excess liquid.
  2. In a bowl, mix the drained tuna, mayonnaise, finely chopped celery, and your choice of seasonings (e.g., salt, pepper, paprika).
  3. Spread the tuna salad mixture generously onto a slice of bread.
  4. Top the tuna salad with fresh lettuce and slices of tomato.
  5. Place another slice of bread on top to create a sandwich.
  6. Enjoy your delicious and healthy tuna salad sandwich!
