Peanut Butter Banana Toast

A great Peanut butter banana toast pre workout!

Peanut butter banana toast is a delightful and energizing choice for a quick breakfast or snack. This recipe features natural peanut butter generously spread on toasted whole-grain bread. It's then adorned with slices of fresh banana and a drizzle of honey for a touch of natural sweetness. With a combination of protein, healthy fats, and carbohydrates, this toast provides a delicious and nutritious option for muscle recovery and growth.

Ingredients

  • Natural peanut butter
  • Whole-grain bread
  • Banana
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Take a slice or two of whole-grain bread and toast them to your preferred level of crispiness.
  2. Spread a generous amount of natural peanut butter on the toasted bread.
  3. Slice a banana and arrange the banana slices on top of the peanut butter.
  4. For added sweetness, drizzle a bit of honey over the banana slices.
  5. Put the second slice of toast on top if you're making a sandwich or simply enjoy an open-faced version.
  6. This quick and tasty peanut butter banana toast is packed with protein, healthy fats, and carbohydrates, making it an excellent choice for muscle recovery and growth.
